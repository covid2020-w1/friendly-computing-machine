Let's learn about rap

Lil Wayne, born Dwayne Michael Carter Jr., is a highly influential rapper, songwriter, and record executive known for his distinctive raspy voice and lyrical prowess. He rose to fame at a young age, becoming a prominent figure in the hip-hop industry with hits like "Lollipop" and "A Milli." Lil Wayne's prolific career, marked by numerous Grammy awards and collaborations with top artists, has solidified his status as one of the most iconic figures in rap music.

  • Lil Wayne signed with Cash Money Records at just 11 years old, becoming its youngest member.
  • He's known for releasing a multitude of popular mixtapes, including the "Dedication" series.
  • Lil Wayne is an enthusiastic skateboarder and has his own skate park and clothing line dedicated to the sport.

Lil Wayne is from New Orleans.
